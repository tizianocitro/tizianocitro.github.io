Experience

  1. Software Engineer @ CeRICT

    May 2023 — Present

    I was part of a project funded by the European Commission, CS-AWARE NEXT, with the CS-AWARE Corporation, Fujitsu, and many other parties from several places worldwide. The project fosters awareness and collaboration in Cybersecurity management within organizations and ecosystems, where an ecosystem is a series of organizations in the same supply chain.

    My contribution was designing and developing the CS-Connect collaboration platform, which allows to:
    – Share and collaborate on Cyber Threat Intelligence and Cybersecurity-related information sourced from government agencies, public administrations, blogs, social media, and other channels.
    – Manage organizational assets through a system graph, which delineates assets and their connections.
    – Understand and navigate relationships with other parties in the supply chain via an ecosystem graph, which models the interconnectedness between organizations.
    – Collaboratively create security, business continuity, and disaster recovery policies.

    CS-Connect was developed by customizing the Mattermost open-source collaboration platform using React, TypeScript, and Go and then deployed within Docker containers on Amazon Web Services. Cyber Threat Intelligence was exchanged using STIX, while policies and courses of action for Cybersecurity operations were implemented via CACAO playbooks.

    Before its deployment in production, CS-Connect underwent testing through pilots and real-user interactions in numerous in-person and remote workshops, during which I conducted demos and presentations.
